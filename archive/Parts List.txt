http://www.digikey.com/product-detail/en/ATMEGA328P-PU/ATMEGA328P-PU-ND/1914589
http://www.atmel.com/devices/atmega328p.aspx

The "P" is short for Pico Power
Note that there is usually a 2nd suffix after the "P", i.e. ATmega328P-PU
The 2nd suffix denotes further information such as extreme temperature tolerance or package type, see the Atmel link above for a full list
Get a PDIP (Plastic Dual Inline Package) package type, other package types will require surface mount soldering

The ATmega328P-PU is the specific chip type used in the video
